About

Oxford Python are an inclusive group who have a passion for the Python programming language .

We are lucky to have a diverse range of members which include hobbyists, proffessional programmers, scientists who use Python both professionally and personally.

We welcome beginners, experienced programmers and non programmers to our events which include social meetups , talks and coding sessions .

Our aim is to provide a space where anyone can contribute and include a Code of Conduct for all of our events.
